Help & FAQ

01

PRAYER TIMES

Q: My prayer times seem wrong.

A: Go to Settings and check your calculation method. Different regions use different methods:

  • MWL (Muslim World League) — Europe, Far East
  • ISNA — North America
  • Egyptian — Africa, Middle East
  • Umm al-Qura — Saudi Arabia, Gulf
  • Karachi — South Asia

Also check that the correct Asr school is selected (Shafi'i or Hanafi).

Q: Prayer times don't update.

A: Make sure location permission is granted. Open Settings > Privacy > Location Services and ensure oSalah is set to "While Using the App." If GPS is unavailable, the app uses your last known location.

02

QIBLA COMPASS

Q: The compass is spinning or inaccurate.

A: Your device's magnetometer may need calibration. Move your phone slowly in a figure-8 pattern several times. Avoid using the compass near metal objects, magnets, or electronic devices.

Q: The compass shows "Unavailable."

A: Some devices or protective cases may interfere with the compass sensor. Remove any magnetic case and try again.

03

QURAN READER

Q: How do I change the font size?

A: Go to More > Prayer Settings and adjust the Quran font size slider.

Q: How do I switch translations?

A: While reading a surah, tap the language buttons (EN / FR / HA) at the top of the reader. You can also toggle translations on or off with the eye icon.

04

DHIKR COUNTER

Gestures:

  • Tap or swipe up — Count
  • Swipe down — Reset
  • Swipe left — Next phrase
  • Swipe right — Previous phrase
  • Long press — Show meaning
05

ZAKAT CALCULATOR

Q: What is nisab?

A: Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must possess for zakat to be obligatory. oSalah uses the gold standard: 85 grams of gold at current market value.

Q: Is the gold price accurate?

A: The gold price is an approximate reference value. For precise zakat calculations, verify the current gold price for your region and consult a local scholar.
