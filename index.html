COMMON QUESTIONS

How much does a professional website cost?+

Our websites start at $100 (₦160,000) for a landing page with contact form and hosting. Multi-page business sites with SEO and branding start at $250. Custom sites with payment integration and admin panels start at $500. Every project includes mobile optimization and 30-day support.

How long does it take to build a website or app?+

Most websites are designed, built, and launched in 7 days. Complex projects with custom features take 14 days. Mobile apps typically take 2–4 weeks depending on scope. We’ll give you a clear timeline before starting.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?+

Yes. Every project includes 30 days of free support. After that, our Managed Presence plans start at $15/month and include hosting, updates, and technical support. Premium plans add SEO, social media, and strategy calls.

What technologies do you use?+

We use modern, production-grade technologies: Next.js and React for web, Flutter for mobile (iOS + Android from one codebase), Rust for backend services, and PostgreSQL for data. Everything is optimized for speed and reliability.

Can you build custom business tools?+

Absolutely. We build invoicing systems, inventory trackers, customer management tools, POS systems, booking platforms, and custom dashboards. If your business has a workflow, we can digitize it.
